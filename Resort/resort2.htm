Elegant, exclusive and in its essence a state of mind, the Sannidhya Resort is an exquisite beach getaway located in Alibaug-Raigarh, a beautiful ocean's district in Maharashtra, a state in the West-south of India. Situated beneath an enormous canopy of a lush tropical rainforest, journey into nature's wonderland - in the company of amphibians, birds, fish, mammals, reptiles, flowering plants and butterflies that inhabit the dense Karnala rainforest. You will be seduced by the promise of discovery, bliss and exaltation - body, mind and soul.
